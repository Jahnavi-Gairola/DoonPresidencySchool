DOON PRESIDENCY SCHOOL

Doon Presidency School in Dehradun is affiliated to the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations (ICSE). This ICSE School in Dehradun is known for its quality education and facilities. Computer labs, science labs and playground are palces of interest for the students where they can learn more things.

why choose us?

Doon Presidency School prepares students for life, helping them develop an informed curiosity and a lasting passion for learning. Our can shape a curriculum around how they want their students to learn, helping them discover new abilities and a wider world. Our students develop the skills they need to achieve at school, university and work. Doon Presidency prides itself on academic achievement and outcomes, global integration, faculty excellence, and a personalized approach to teaching and learning. We place a strong emphasis on the core values of safety, responsibility and respect and encourage a positive approach to learning. We use a range of strategies to assist students to take responsibility for their own learning and behaviour.

Our school vision clearly states our intention to educate leaders of the future in an environment that both challenges and supports. We aim to prepare students to live in and contribute to a sustainable global economy. We are committed to educating children academically, socially, emotionally and physically. We hope learners from Doon Presidency School will be adaptable problem solvers, environmentally responsible and capable of dealing with the demands of their lives today and in the future.

Our P&C (Parents & Community) Association have a long history of contributing economically to school operations and facilities. The efforts of the Association are invaluable and offer parents practical ways to support fundraising initiatives for investment back into school programs and resources. We operate a Helping Hands parent volunteer program and encourage able parents to contribute time and effort in the classroom where appropriate.
